Spores

Spores is a simple casual game where you play as a "Spore" and try to eat other "Spores" to grow in size with the goal to not get eaten by other deadly Spores.

You can play Spores on standalone & web on Itch.io using mouse controls and/or play Spores on your Android device through the google play store.

Tilting Ship

Tilting Ship is a casual mobile game where you attempt to evade increasingly faster moving obstacles going in your way.

You can play Tilting Ships on standalone from Itch.io using key controls and/or play on your Android device through the google play store.

[Tilting Ship on Itch.io]

Reactangle

Reactangle is a small reaction game initally written in less than 250 lines of code within 1 script. It provides 3 different game modes to test your reaction speed, through increasingly speeding up the pace of the game with every point gained.

You can play Reactangles on standalone and web from Itch.io using keyboard & mouse controls. Reactangle is currently not available on the google play store.
