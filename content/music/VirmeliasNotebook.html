Verse 1

She’s the kind who lights up the room,

With a laugh that can chase away gloom.

I’m just the guy who’s there by her side,

Helping her through when the answers hide.


We share quiet moments, a book and a pen,

She doesn’t see it, but she’s my best friend.

Not looking for love, not chasing a spark,

Just hoping one day she’ll hear my heart.
