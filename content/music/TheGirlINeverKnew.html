The Girl I Never Knew

Based on true events.

Verse 1

He was a boy, so tender, so shy,

Dreamed of the stars, reached for the sky.

Then she appeared, a name on a screen,

A face he had pictured but never had seen.


Her words were soft, her laughter was sweet,

She made his heart skip every beat.

Innocent chats turned into the night,

Two souls were dancing in digital light.

Chorus 1

Oh, he fell for a girl he had never met,

Lost in her voice, no room for regret.

But love's a fire, it burns or it heals,

And not every love is as true as it feels.

Verse 2

They met at last, the world stood still,

Time seemed to stop, against their will.

Her touch was warm, her smile was bright,

The boy believed in love at first sight.


But cracks appeared, a shadow, a shade,

Her kindness a mask that slowly decayed.

She read his words, accused him of lies,

Her anger was fire, her love a disguise.

Verse 3

Her fists would fly, her voice would roar,

But he stayed silent, would not go to war.

He bore the pain, the bruises, the shame,

Blinded by love, he carried the blame.


The whispers grew louder, the truth came clear,

Friends pulled him close, said, "She’s not sincere."

And though his heart cracked under the weight,

He found the strength to walk from his fate.

Chorus 2

Oh, he fell for a girl he had never met,

But love turned bitter, he paid the debt.

Verse 4 (Ending)

She begged, she cried, she schemed, she lied,,

But he’d seen the truth, no longer denied.

His heart was shattered, the pieces askew,

But he knew at last what he had to do.


He let her go, though it tore him apart,

A lesson in love, a scar on his heart.

For sometimes the ones we think we adore,

Are the ones we must leave to find something more.
