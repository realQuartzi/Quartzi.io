Destiny's Whisper

Part 1: Her Story

Verse 1

I met him on a rainy day,

His smile chased all the clouds away.

He held my heart, but didn’t know,

He makes me feel like I was home.

Pre Chorus 1

Every moment felt so bright,

He turned my darkness into light.

But the wind carried him away,

And he left without a word to say.

Chorus (Her)

Why does destiny let us meet,

When we’re bound to face defeat?

He was my calm, my gentle sea,

But now he's just a memory.

Verse 2

I wait where we once used to stand,

Traced his touch, on my hand.

He gave me care I’d never known

Then vanished... now I'm all alone.

Outro (Her)

Did he want to stay, but couldn’t fight?

Was it fate that stole him from my sight?

I’ll never know why he walked away,

But I whisper his name every day.

Part 2: His Story

Verse 3

She laughed like the world was hers,

Her voice, the sweetest song I’ve heard.

We painted skies with dreams so high,

I never thought we’d say goodbye.

Pre Chorus 2

We walked through forests, chasing streams,

Her love was brighter than my dreams.

But whispers told her I’m too good

And she let go, thought I was no good.

Chorus (Him)

Why does destiny let us meet,

When we’re bound to face defeat?

She was my fire, my gentle flame,

But now she’s just a friend I claim.

Verse 4

I see her face in every crowd,

Her laughter echoes, far too loud.

They told her lies, she believed their song,

And now I’m left where I don’t belong.

Outro (Him)

Did she want to stay, but couldn’t fight?

Did they steal her heart and blur her sight?

I’ll never know why she turned away,

But I whisper her name every day.

Part 3: Both

Bridge

(Her) I never told him how I cared.

(Him) I wish I’d shown her I was scared.


(Together)

Two hearts aligned, a perfect tune,

But torn apart by fate too soon.

Chorus (Together)

Why does destiny let us meet,

When we’re bound to face defeat?

A love so pure, it felt divine,

But it wasn’t ours, it’s not our time.

Outro (Together)

And so, we’re left to dream alone,

Two strangers lost, forever unknown.
