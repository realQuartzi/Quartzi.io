The Puppet Master

She is iresistable

Verse 1

Black heels,

Big deal,

Spinning in a tight reel.

Sharp grin,

Pulls me in,

She always knows how to win.


Red lips,

Ice kiss,

Leaving trails I can’t dismiss.

Wide eyes,

Alibis,

Playing games I didn’t realize.

Pre Chorus

She says, "Take my hand, baby, follow my lead",

But every step she takes leaves me weak at the knees.

She’s a venom in silk, a fire that burns,

Every twist of her smile, another lesson I’ve learned.

Chorus

Oh, how she moves, how she plays,

Turning my nights into poisonous days.

Her smile’s a dagger, her touch is a chain,

I’m caught in her fire, addicted to the pain.


And when she laughs, it’s the sound of defeat,

Every victory she claims tastes bitter and sweet.

I’m breaking, I’m shaking, but she holds the key,

The only escape is when she sets me free.

Verse 2

Dark eyes,

Disguise,

Pulling strings in plain sight.

Soft lies,

Sharp bite,

Breaking me in every fight.


She leans,

She preens,

Paints the world like it’s a scene.

No shame,

Deadly game,

But I still play it all the same.

Chorus

Oh, how she moves, how she plays,

Turning my nights into poisonous days.

Her smile’s a dagger, her touch is a chain,

I’m caught in her fire, addicted to the pain.


And when she laughs, it’s the sound of defeat,

Every victory she claims tastes bitter and sweet.

I’m breaking, I’m shaking, but she holds the key,

The only escape is when she sets me free.

Bridge

She twists the knife while she watches me bleed,

Feeding her hunger, the chaos she needs.

But the moment she tires, the moment she’s done,

She drops the strings, and I come undone.

Chorus End

Oh, how she moved, how she played,

Turned my nights into poisonous days.

Her smile was a dagger, her touch was a chain,

Now I walk in her shadow, bound to the pain.


When she laughed, it was the sound of defeat,

Her power still lingers, dark and discreet.

She broke me, she shaped me, but I hold the key,

The scars will remind me, but now I’m finally free.
