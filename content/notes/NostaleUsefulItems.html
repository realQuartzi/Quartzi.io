List of Items to keep in Nostale

This is a list of Items you probably want to keep in Nostale (in my opinion).

This list is mainly made for Olympus, if something does not exist, it is probably not worth keeping in my opinion for the most likely the wrong reasons.

I am using NosApki for the image source. Thank you <3


Tips and Hints

Equipment

Since this is based on Olympus. I highly recommend not crafting any items that are not Act 9.

You are given C60 gear as a reward to reaching C60. This should get you through Land of Life (LoL) all the way to Act9 C83+. (It did for me)

Fairies

Do not waste your time with buying an expensive Fairy. Any thing past Tier 2 (Grown) is a waste of money. You will be able to get to 70% Fairy Power, which is good enough.

Getting a Varik, Erenia, Zenas, Fernon, etc. is a waste of Millions/Billions. When you reach C90 you can run Act 9 raids, and get the new Steam Drone Fairies from the Raid Box. It is not worth crafting them.

Wings

Untill you are C90 any sort of "5% damage" boost will not help you. There are more important things to throw your money at, like a good C83-C90 Equipment. Or upgrading your Equipment to C90.

Resistances

Do not buy new Resistances untill you have good equipment. The resistances you get as a reward for leveling up are good enough untill you can afford wasting money at C90.

General Tip

Don't bother buying anything untill you are C90... Something that might actually be worth, is paying for Experience Boost at C81 in Act9 (preferably at C83+ since LoL can get us pretty far at a good pace).

Run Marathons for money. I ran: Fernon, Namaju, Act 7 Marathon Raids for money. This way I got a lot that I can spend on extra things. And I never open my boxes, I always sell them in Bazar (even Elementals).
